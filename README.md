# Zepto E-Commerce SQL Data Analysis — Business Questions

This document outlines the key business problems solved using SQL on the Zepto e-commerce dataset, as part of a data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and business analytics workflow in PostgreSQL.

## Data Exploration
- Counted total records in the dataset
- Reviewed sample data and column structure
- Checked for null values across all key columns
- Identified distinct product categories
- Compared in-stock vs out-of-stock product counts
- Identified products listed multiple times under different SKUs

## Data Cleaning
- Identified and removed records with an MRP of zero
- Converted price fields from paise to rupees for accurate analysis

## Business Analysis Questions

1. **Top 10 Best-Value Products** — Find the top 10 products offering the highest discount percentage.
2. **High MRP, Out-of-Stock Products** — Identify high-value products (MRP > ₹300) that are currently out of stock.
3. **Estimated Revenue by Category** — Calculate the estimated revenue generated by each product category.
4. **Premium Products with Low Discounts** — Find products with an MRP above ₹500 but a discount of less than 10%.
5. **Top 5 Categories by Average Discount** — Identify the five categories offering the highest average discount percentage.
6. **Best Value by Weight** — Calculate price per gram for products above 100g to determine the best value-for-money options.
7. **Product Weight Segmentation** — Classify products into Low, Medium, and Bulk weight categories.
8. **Total Inventory Weight by Category** — Calculate the total inventory weight for each product category.

---

*Dataset: Zepto e-commerce inventory data | Tool: PostgreSQL*
